---

## 🧠 Why use `useReducer`?

For complex states like Cart/Wishlist, `useReducer()` is better than `useState()`:

| State | useState | useReducer |
|-------|----------|------------|
| Simple toggles | ✅ | ❌ |
| Arrays of items with actions | ❌ | ✅ |
| Add/Remove/Update logic | ❌ | ✅ |
